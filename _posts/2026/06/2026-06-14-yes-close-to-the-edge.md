## Jim's Notes

- One of the great Yes albums.
- The organ part during the I Get Up I Get Down portion of Close to the Edge is kinda jarring to me but otherwise one of the best songs to take up the entire A side of a rock album.
- Not picking a cut because all 3 songs are very good to great and I cannot see dropping any of them.
- Yes lyrics often don't make sense. I'm looking at you: "We hear the total mass retain." Yeah, it's all metaphors but WTF?!?

## Chris's Notes

Cutting a song from this album is a bit silly, so think of this is as a forced ranking. The album is perfect as it is.

Side 1 is addicting: great hooks, ear worm lyrics, lyrical beauty. Julie just finished *Siddhartha* and I read a few years ago. Spending more time parsing the lyrics.

Siberian Khatru is fine - lacking finesse of rest of the album. More organ-progressive than I like.
