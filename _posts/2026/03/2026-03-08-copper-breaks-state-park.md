Second stop in Texas and sixth stop overall on our way out west. Two nights here before we begin our final push to the Grand Canyon. Copper Breaks sits a little more than halfway to Amarillo from Dallas. Some decent hiking here. A great place for stargazing since there is very little light pollution.
